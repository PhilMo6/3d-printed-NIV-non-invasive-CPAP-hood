While printing door part pay attention and pause half way, you must insert 1/4" non latex dental elastics into the 4 outside posts before you resume.
Once all parts are printed insert arms of slide into the door elastics until bands fit into gooves of slides.
Make sure bands are not twisted or wrapped around each other. 
Insert slide first followed by the door with the top of the door fitting aganst the groove of the main housing.
Screw cap onto the housing until desired tightness.
